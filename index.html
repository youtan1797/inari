About Inari

The journey of returning what we've borrowed from the Earth

Our Origin

Inari was born in the high-altitude deserts of Ladakh, where the thin air and rugged mountains taught us the true meaning of resilience. Founded by Mohd in 2025, our mission began with a simple promise made among Himalayan winds.

The Mission

We exist to restore balance between humanity and nature. For every product sold, we plant native seeds in degraded ecosystems. Our goal is to rewild 1,000 hectares of Himalayan land by 2030 through community-led initiatives.

How We Work

Inari partners with local communities, especially women's cooperatives, to grow and distribute native seeds. We combine traditional knowledge with modern technology to create sustainable solutions that honor the Earth.

Our Products

From Himalayan-grown sprouts to eco-packaging solutions, every Inari product is designed with regeneration in mind. Our current focus is on Changpa Kaizen sprouts, grown using ancient techniques adapted for modern sustainability.

The Future

By 2026, we aim to establish seed banks across 10 Himalayan communities. Our vision includes developing a mobile app that tracks each seed's journey from planting to growth, connecting consumers directly with restoration efforts. The Inari promise continues to grow, one seed at a time.
